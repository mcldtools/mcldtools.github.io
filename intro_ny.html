Zoyenera kudziwa zokhudza Chipangizo Cholingira ngati ma pologalamu a chitukuko akutsogoleredwa ndi anthu eni ake chotchedwa CLD Assessment Tool

  • Ngati mufuna kudziwa zambiri kapena kupereka maganizo anu, lembelani Gunjan Veda wa The Movement for Community-led Development pa keyala iyi: gunjan.veda@thp.org

Chiyambi:

Chipangizochi chakonzedza kudzera mu ngwirizano wa akadaulo a kafukufuku ochokera ku The Movement for Community-led Development. Ngati mukufuna kunva zambiri zokhudza omwe anakonza chipangizochi komanso momwe mungachigwiritsire ntchito mungazipeze powerenga

Mndandanda uwu

Chidule cha Mawu:

  • CLD - Community-led Development
  • M&E - Monitoring and Evaluation
  • Njira zounikira udindo ndi kukwaniritsa udindo: Izi ndi njira kapena ukadaulo omwe umakhudzana ndi za kugawana zotsatira za kauniuni kugawidwa kwa onse okhudzidwa, kulengeza ziganizo zomwe zinatengedwa komanso kagwiritsidwe ntchtio kandalama ndi onse okhudzidwa kudzera malo owonekera kugulu, m`misonkhano yosiyanasiyana yokhudza komanso yomwe anthu onse amudera amatenga nawo gawo
  • Kadaulo otsogolera zokambirana mudera (Fasiliteta)- Awa ndi anthu omwe amagwira ntchito mudera ndipo amayan`ganira ma pulogalamu komanso kutsogolera ndandanda ndi kachitidwe ka ntchito pamodzi ndi anthu adera. Iwo atha kukhala ogwira ntchito ndi boma, mabungwe kapenanso anthu adera ogwira ntchito modzipeleka

Chpangizochi angachigwiritse ntchito ndiyani ndipo angachigwiritse ntchito motani kapena nyengo ziti?

  • Oyendetsa ntchito za pulojekiti (Project Officer), kuphatikizapo owunika momwe pulojekiti ikuyendera(Monitoring and Evaluation) komanso ena ndi ena omwe amagwira ntchito za chitukuko omwe akudziwa bwino za momwe pulojekiti imayendera, zotsatira za pulojekitiyo komanso masomphenya ake. Chipangizo chino chikhonzanso kulembedwa mothandizana pakati pa oyendetsa pulojekiti ndi atsogoleri a kudera.
  • Mabungwe atha kugwiritsa ntchito chipangizochi pa nthawi zosiyasiyana mu pulojekiti, monga pamene akukonza kumene pulojekiti yawo, pamene akuzukuta momwe pulojekiti ikuyendera, kapenanso pamene akutsendera kumapeto kwa pulojekiti. Cholinga chake ndi kufuna kuzukuta ngati pulojekiti yawo ikukwaniritsa zoyiyenereza kutchedwa “pulojekiti yotsogozedwa ndi anthu eni ake kapena kuti Community Led”.
  • Mabungwe akhonzanso kugwiritsa ntchito chipangizo ichi pamene akuchita kawuniwuni wa momwe akuyendetsera kapena kachitidwe kawu muma pologalamu osiyanasiyana

Kulongosola magawo a m'chipangizochi:

  1. Gawo lachidziwitso: Gawoli limasonkhanitsa zidziwitso za yemwe adalemba fomuyo, nthawi yomwe izi zidachitika, ndi zofotokozera zina zilizonse zomwe bungwe lingafune kuti zigwiritsidwe ntchito mtsogolo.
  2. Gawo lolowetsa zizindikiro ndi kachititidwe ka CLD: Gawoli limasonkhanitsa zambiri za zindikiro za CLD ndi machitidwe ake
  3. Gawo la zotsatira: Gawoli likupeleka chidule cha zotsatira za momwe zizindikiro and kachitidwe ka CLD kalili
  4. Data: Kusonyeza njira za momwe mungatumizire zotsatira, kuitanitsa zotsatira kapena kufufuta zomwe mwalowetsa mu gawo la Zizindikiro and kachitidwe ka CLD

Kodi muyeso uno tiwulembe bwanji?

  • >Gawo lililonse la muyesowu lili ndi mfundo zosiyanasiyana komanso mayankho omwe anthu angapeleke pa mfundozo
  • >Yankho lililonse lili ndi zigoli zake, motere: (0) kuyimira kuti “Palibepo umboni wokwanira; (1) kuyimira kuti “Samayesa nkomwe kutero”; (2) kuyimira kuti “Amayeselako kutero”; (3) kuyimira kuti “Apita patsogolo kutero”; (4) kuyimira kuti “Akukwaniritsa”.
  • >Sankhani pa zigoli zisanuzi chigoli chomwe chikuimilira bwino momwe ntchito yomwe bungwe lanu likuchita ikuyendera pa magawo aliwonse a muyesowu, pa kutsogolera zokambirana, komanso pa kawuniwuni ndi kalondolondo
  • >Chipangizochi chizigwiritsa ntchito mayankho omwe alembedwa kuti chijambule pa chokha m’magawo onse zomwe zisonyeza momwe ntchito yake ikuyendela

Kodi mungatanthauzire bwanji zotsatira?

  • Masomphenya a mabungwe omwe akugwiritsa ntchito njira za CLD (ntchito zotsogoleredwa ndi anthu eni ake) akuyenera kukhala ofuna kufika pachimake penipeni m’magawo onse a chipangizochi mogwirizana ndi ntchito zawo,dera komanso kagwiridwe kawo kantchito
  • Sizonse zili m’chipangizochi zingakhale zomwe zikufunikira kwambiri pa ma pulojekiti osiyanasiyana; komabe, pa zomwe zingakhale zofunikirazo, mabungwe angathandizike kwambiri pozukuta momwe zithunzi zomwe chipangizochi chingatulutse m’magawo ake ndi kupeza njira zokonzela pomwe zikuwoneka kuti sakuchita bwino.
  • Gawo la zotsatira: likuwumba mkota wa zigoli zonse zomwe taika pa muyeso uja
  • Kulongosola magawo achipangizochi: Chipangizochi chili ndi magawo ang`ono ang`ono pa A nd G (mu gawo lolongosora muyezo wa kakhalidwe ka CLD) komanso machitidwe ake (Kauniuni, kalondolondo komanso Katsogozedwe ka zokambirana) ndi cholinga chofuna kutambasula momwe zimenezi zingamachitikire pa bungwe lathu. Magawo apaderawa tawawonjezera muma galafu 4 ndi 5.
