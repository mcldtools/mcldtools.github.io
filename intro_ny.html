Zoyenera kudziwa zokhudza Chipangizo Cholingira ngati ma pologalamu a chitukuko akutsogoleredwa ndi anthu eni ake chotchedwa CLD Assessment Tool Ngati mufuna kudziwa zambiri kapena kupereka maganizo anu , lembelani Gunjan Veda wa The Movement for Community-led Development pa keyala iyi: gunjan.veda@thp.org Chiyambi: "Chipangizochi chakonzedza kudzera mu ngwirizano wa aka fukufuku ochokera ku The Movement for Community-led Development. Ngati mukufuna kunva zambiri zokhudza omwe anakonza chipangizochi komanso momwe mungachigwiritsire ntchito mungazipeze powerenga mlozo uwu This tool has been developed as part of the Collaborative Research by The Movement for Community-led Development. For further information on who developed it and how to use it, see this Guidance document" Chidule cha Mawu: "▪CLD - Community-led Development ▪M&E - Monitoring and Evaluation" Chpangizochi angachigwiritse ntchito ndiyani ndipo angachigwiritse ntchito motani kapena nyengo ziti? Who should complete this tool and when? "▪Oyendetsa ntchito za pulojekiti (Project Officer), kuphatikizapo owunika momwe pulojekiti ikuyendera(Monitoring and Evaluation) komanso ena ndi ena omwe amagwira ntchito za chitukuko omwe akudziwa bwino za momwe pulojekiti imayendera, zotsatira za pulojekitiyo komanso masomphenya ake. Chipangizo chino chikhonzanso kulembedwa mothandizana pakati pa oyendetsa pulojekiti ndi atsogoleri a kudera. • Mabungwe atha kugwiritsa ntchito chipangizochi pa nthawi zosiyasiyana mu pulojekiti, monga pamene akukonza kumene pulojekiti yawo, pamene akuzukuta momwe pulojekiti ikuyendera, kapenanso pamene akutsendera kumapeto kwa pulojekiti. Cholinga chake ndi kufuna kuzukuta ngati pulojekiti yawo ikukwaniritsa zoyiyenereza kutchedwa “pulojekiti yotsogozedwa ndi anthu eni ake kapena kuti Community Led”. • Mabungwe akhonzanso kugwiritsa ntchito chipangizo ichi pamene akuchita kawuniwuni wa momwe akuyendetsera kapena kachitidwe kawu muma pologalamu osiyanasiyana" Kulongosola magawo a m'chipangizochi "Chipangizochi chili ndi magawo asanu (5) awa: - GAWO 1 – MUTU kapena Chiyambi – Gawo ili ndi limene timalembapo mwachidule zokhudza omwe amagwiritsa ntchito chipangizochi, monga: dzina la munthuyo; tsiku lomwe tikulemba mchipangizochi; ndi zina zomwe bungwe lingafune kulemba kuti lidzathe kukumbukira mtsogolo. - GAWO 2 - MUYESO – Gawo ili lili m’magawo awiri – Onani momwe mungalembere za m’magawo awiriwa powerengaa Mfuna ndi anthu 1) Kutoluera nfundo zomwe zimakhazikitsa kuti pologalamu ikakhale yotsata ndodnomekoza chitukuko chotsogozedwa ndi anthu ya CLD 2) kutorela Mfuno zokhudza ndondomeko ya chitukuko chotsogodzedwa ndi anthu - GAWO 3 - ZOTSATIRA – Gawo ili ndilimene limawumba mkota/kusonkhanitsa zotsatira zonse za muyeso muchipangizochi GAWO 4 – Nthambi 1_ Zizindikiro – Gawo ili ndi lomwe likutambasula mndandanda wonse wa magawo a zizindikiro za ntchito za chitukuko chotsogozedwa ndi anthu eni ake GAWO 5 – Nthambi 2_ Ndondomeko - Gawo ili ndilomwe likutambasula kachitidwe ka kawuniwuni ndi kalondolondo pa chitukuko chotsogozedwa ndi anthu, komanso zokhudza kutsogolera zokambirana pomwe tikugwiritsa ntchito chipangizochi" Kodi muyeso uno tiwulembe bwanji (GAWO 2)? "Gawo lililonse la muyesowu lili ndi mfundo zosiyanasiyana komanso mayankho omwe anthu angapeleke pa mfundozo Yankho lililonse lili ndi zigoli zake, motere: (0) kuyimira kuti “Palibepo umboni wokwanira; (1) kuyimira kuti “Samayesa nkomwe kutero”; (2) kuyimira kuti “Amayeselako kutero”; (3) kuyimira kuti “Apita patsogolo kutero”; (4) kuyimira kuti “Akukwaniritsa”. Sankhani pa zigoli zisanuzi chigoli chomwe chikuimilira bwino momwe ntchito yomwe bungwe lanu likuchita ikuyendera pa magawo aliwonse a muyesowu, pa kutsogolera zokambirana, komanso pa kawuniwuni ndi kalondolondo  Onetsetsani kuti mwayika zigoli zanu mu ma bokosi otuwa omwe ali ndi chizinikiro cha ‘H’ pamwamba pake  Oselemba zigoli zanu m’mizere yobiriwira  Pomwe mukulemba zigolizi, chipangizo chino chiziwonkhetsa chokha zotsatira za zigoli zanu mu mizere yobiriwira.  Chipangizochi chizigwiritsa ntchito mayankho omwe alembedwa kuti chijambule pa chokha m’magawo 3 mpaka 5 zinthuzi zosonyeza momwe ntchito yake ikuyendela " Titanthauzire bwanji zotsatira za mu gawo 3 mpaka 5? " Masomphenya a mabungwe omwe akugwiritsa ntchito njira za CLD (ntchito zotsogoleredwa ndi anthu eni ake) akuyenera kukhala ofuna kufika pachimake penipeni m’magawo onse a chipangizochi mogwirizana ndi ntchito zawo ndi madela awo.  Sizonse zili m’chipangizo chino zomwe zingakhale zofunikira kwambiri pa ma pulojekiti athu; komabe, pa zomwe zingakhale zofunikirazo, mabungwe angathandizike kwambiri pozukuta momwe zithunzi zomwe chipangizochi chingatulutse m’magawo ake ndi kupeza njira zokonzela pomwe zikuwoneka kuti sakuchita bwino.  Gawo 3 – Zotsatira: likuwumba mkota wa zigoli zonse zomwe taika pa muyeso uja  Gawo 4 ndi 5 – magawo ang’onoang’ono mwa tsatanetsatane: Gawo lino likuphwanya magawo akuluakulu aja komanso ndondomeko za kawuniwuni ndi kalondolondolondo ndinso kutsogolera zokambirana ndi cholinga chofuna kutambasula momwe zimenezi zingachitikire pa bungwe lathu. Zithuzi zomwe chipangizochi chikutulutsa mmagawo 4 ndi 5 ndi zothandizira kuti Mfundo zofunikira zomwe takambirana polemba chipangizochi zisatayike. "
