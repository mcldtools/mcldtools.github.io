Chida Chowunikira kugwirila ntchito limodzi cha CLD (cha mtundu wa kumudzi) mwachidule

  • Kuti mumve zambiri kapena kupereka ndemanga, lemberani kwa awa: Mkulu wa Kafukufuku, Gunjan Veda, ku Movement for Community-led Development (CLD): gunjan.veda@mcld.org

Mbiri yake

Chida Chowunikira Chothandizira cha CLD chinapangidwa ngati gawo la Kafukufuku Wogwirizana ku The Movement for Community-led Development. Kuti mumve zambiri za omwe adapanga komanso momwe mungagwiritsire ntchito, onani chikalata ichi cha Malangizo.

Mu chaka cha 2021, gulu la akadaulo padziko lonse lapansi adagwira ntchito limodzi kutambasula chidachi ndicholinga choti chisavute kagwiritsidwe ntchito ndi anthu akumudzi. Chifukwa chake, mtundu wa Community wa chidacho unabadwa. Zilibe mawu ophiphilitsa komanso ofuna masulira. Ngati muli ndi ndemanga pa chidachi, kutengera momwe chimagwiritsidwira ntchito ndi anthu ammudzi, chonde tithandizeni.

Kafotokozedwe:

  • CLD - Chitukuko chotsogozedwa ndi anthu onse
  • M&E - Kuyang'anira ndi Kafukufuku
  • Njira zoyika kalikonse poyera ndi kufotokezera bwino kuphatikizapo kugawana malipoti owunika, kuwerengera zisankho zawo ndikugwiritsa ntchito ndalama kudzera munjira zachidzwitso za anthu komanso kudzera m'misonkhano yolumikizana monga mabwalo a anthu mwachitsanzo. kafukufuku wa chikhalidwe cha anthu kapena maphunziro a kotala kapena kawiri pachaka komwe madera onse angayimire, ndi zina zotero,
  • Otsogolera - ogwira ntchito m'bungwe kapena mapologalamu omwe amafotokoza za pulogalamuyo ndikugwira ntchito ndi anthu tsiku ndi tsiku kuti athe kukonza mapulani ndi kuchitapo kanthu. Otsogolera atha kugwira ntchito m'mabungwe omwe siaboma, kukhala odzipereka ammudzi, ndi zina zotero.

Ndindani amene akhoza kugwiritsa ntchito chidachi ndipo liti?

  • Chida ichi ndi cha ogwira ntchito (ma ofesala), akatswiri a zowelengera ndi kafukufuku (M&E), ndi akatswiri omwe akumvetsetsa bwino za mmene angayendetsere chitgukuko,kunvetsetsa za zotsatsarira chitukuko komanso ntchito ikagwirika, posatengera kuti zinalingaliridwa kapena zinayezedwa kale. Iyenera kugwiritsidwa ntchito limodzi ndi atsogoleri ammudzi ndi ogwira ntchito omwe amatsogolera chitukukocho.
  • Mabungwe atha kugwiritsa ntchito chida ichi pa nthawi zosiyanasiyana papulogalamu kuti adziwonetse okha ngati mapulogalamu amagwirizanirana ndi ndondomeka za CLD - panthawi ya mapangidwe, ndemanga zapakati pa pulogalamu, kapena kumapeto kwa pulogalamu yodziwitsa mapulogalamu amtsogolo.
  • Mabungwe akhoza kugwiritsanso ntchito chidachi panthawi yomwe akuwunika bungwe lao kuti amvetsetse mapulogalamu omwe amapanga m'madera onse.

Kufotokozera Magawo:

  1. Chidziwitso Chachikulu: Imasonkhanitsa zidziwitso za yemwe adalemba fomuyo, nthawi yomwe izi zidachitika, ndi zofotokozera zina zilizonse zomwe bungwe lingafune kuti zigwiritsidwe ntchito mtsogolo.
  2. Rubriki imasonkhanitsa zambiri za makhalidwe a CLD ndi ndondomeko
  3. Masamba a Zotsatira akufotokozera mwachidule zonse, mawonekedwe ndi machitidwe
  4. Data izi zimapeleka mpata wotumiza, kuitanitsa kapena kufufuta zomwe zili mu chipangizo chanu chomwe mugwiritsa ntchito chapafupi.

Kodi mukhoza yuyankha bwani rubriki?

  • Mulingo uliwonse mu rubriki uli ndi ziganizo zosiyanasiyana ndi mayankho ake.
  • Njira zoyankhira (ndi mfundo zofananira) kuphatikiza chidziwitso chosakwanira (0), samayesa (1), amayesa (2), apita patsogolo (3), ndi akwaniritsa(4).
  • Dinani batani lomwe mwa mayankho awa akufotokoza bwino momwe gulu lanu likugwirira ntchito komanso momwe gulu lanu likuyendera pamitundu yosiyanasiyana ya CLD, kutsogolera, ndi njira zakuyan’ganira ndi kafukufuku
  • Rubriki imawerengera yokha mphambu ya muyeso uliwonse ndikuwonetsa pazotsatira

Kodi mungatanthauzire bwanji zotsatira zake?

  • Cholinga cha mabungwe omwe amagwiritsa ntchito njira za CLD chiyenera kukhala kuti pamapeto pake afikire mbali zakunja kuti apeze zigawo zogwirizana ndi zochitika zawo, mapulogalamu awo, ndi machitidwe awo.
  • Si miyeso yonse yomwe ikhoza kukhala yogwirizana ndi mapulogalamu onse, koma pamene ali, malo omwe ali pa graph angathandize mabungwe kuona kummene angapitsire patsogolo mapulogalamu awo.
  • Zotsatira Zonse: Zimasonyeza zotsatira za magawo onse a rubriki.
  • Zigawo: Rubrikiyi imaphatikizaponso kusinthika kwa magawo A - G (makhalidwe a CLD) ndi ndondomeko (za kuyang’anira ndi kuunikira komanso mangatsogoleredwe ake) zomwe zimapereka chidziwitso cha momwe miyeso iyi ingagwiritsidwire ntchito pa bungwe. zogawozi zimaphatikizidwa mu mawonekedwe a graph omwe amapezeka mu tsamba 4 & 5 kuti tsatanetsataneyu asatayike.
  • Chonde onetsetsani kuti mukukambirana zotsatira ndi anthu onse komanso ogwira nawo ntchito mutatha kugwiritsa ntchito chidachi. Kambiranani zofunika kwambiri ndi zomwe mungachite kuti pulogalamu yanu ikhale yotsogozedwa ndi anthu onse okhudzidwa potengera zotsatira za chidachi.
