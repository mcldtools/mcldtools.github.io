Akatiba akakozesebwa okupima enkola y'abantu okwenyigira obutereevu mu nkulaakulana yaabwe.

Ebikwata ku katiba kano mu bufunze

  • Okumanya ebisingawo oba okubaako obubaka bwoweereza, wandiikira Gunjan Veda ow'ebyokunonyereza mu kitongole kya The Movement for Community-led Development (ekitongole ekirafuubana okulaba ng'abantu abawejjere bava mu bwavu): gunjan.veda@mcld.org

Ebikwata ku katiba kano:

Akatiba kano akayambako okupima enkola ey'abantu okwenyigira obutereevu mu nkulakulana yabwe, kaayiyizibwa ekitongole kya The Movement for Community-led Development, ng'ekimu ku kitundu ky'okunonyereza okwakolebwa. Okumanya ebisingawo ku ani yavumbula akatiba kano n'engeri y'okukakozesaamu, tunula mu kiwandiiko kino ekirungamya. Mu 2021, guluupu y'abakugu okwetoloola ensi yonna, baakwatagana ne bakolera wamu ne bongera okugonza akatiba kano, abantu ba bulijjo basobole okukakozesa. Akatiba akakozesebwa abantu ba bulijjo, tekaliimu bigambo bikakali era abantu basobola okukakozesa obulungi. Bwoba olina ky'oyagala okutugamba oba okwebuuza ku nkozesa y'akatiba kano mu bantu ba bulijjo, tutuukirire.

Okutegeera ebigambo:

  • CLD - Community-led Development - Enkulaakulana eyenyigiramu abantu.
  • M&E - Okulondoola n'okupima ebikolebwa.
  • Amakubo agalondoola ebikoleddwa (Accountability Mechanisms) - Mu makubo agalondoola ebikoleddwa mulimu; abakwatibwako okugabanaga lipooti, okunnyonnyola okusalawo okuba kukoleddwa ne ssente ezisaasaanyiziddwa okuyita mu birango eby'olukale, n'enkiiko ez'awamu okugeza ez'okwekeneenya enkuuma y'ebitabo, emisomo egitegekebwa oluvanyuma lw'ebbanga eggere nga abantu bonna bakiikirirwa.
  • Omusomesa (Facilitator) - Omukozi w'ekitongole oba Pulogulaamu, annyonnyola abantu ebikwata ku pulogulaamu n'emirimu buli lunaku okusobola okuteekateeka obulungi n'okuddukanya emirimu. Abasomesa bano basobola okukolera ebibiina by'obwa nakyewa, oba ne bakola nga banakyewa abakola mu bantu ba bulijjo okukyusa embeera zaabwe, n'ebirala.

Ani alina okujjuza akatiba kano era ddi?

  • Akatiba kano ka bakozi, abakugu mu kulondoola n'okupima emirimu egikoleddwa, n'abakozi abalala abategeera engeri pulogulaamu gy'erina okuteekebwa mu nkola, ebinagivaamu, ebisuubirwa oba ebimanyiddwa. Akatiba kano kalina okumalirizibwa mu ngeri ey'okukolaganira awamu n'abakulembeze mu kitundu n'abakozi ba pulogulaamu ekolebwako.
  • Ebitongole bisobola okukozesa akatiba kano ku mitendera egy'enjawulo egya Pulogulaamu gye babeera bakolako, basobole okweteeka ku minzani ku ngeri pulogulaamu gy'etuukanamu n'enkola y'abantu ey'okwenyigira mu nkulaakulana - ku mutendera gw'okugiyiiya, okugyetegereza ng'etuuse mu makkati, oba ku nkomerero y'ayo, okusobola okuyamba okukola obulungi pulogulaamu ezijja mu maaso.
  • Ebitongole nabyo bisobola okukozesa akatiba kano, mu nkiiko z'okwekubamu ttooci, okusobola okutegeera enteekateeka ya pulogulaamu ez'enjawulo.

Okunnyonnyola ebitundu mu katiba kano:

  1. Ebikwata ku muntu: wano wabeerawo ebikwata ku muntu ajjuzza ffoomu, singa abeerawo, kwossa ebikwata ku muntu ebirala, okusinziira ku kitongole bwe kiba kyagala, okusobola okubikozesa mu biseera eby'omu maaso.
  2. Ebikwata ku nteekateeka. Wano wabeerawo ebikwata ku nkola abantu gye bagenda okwenyigiramu obutereevu okusobola okugoba obwavu, ebigikwatako n'emitendera gy'ayo.
  3. Ebivaamu. Wano wabeerawo ebikwata ku Pulogulaamu ku ngeri gy'enatambuzibwamu n'emitendera gyayo mu bufunze.
  4. Ebikunganyiziddwa. Wano waliwo w'osobolera okusindika, okuwanula, oba okusiimuula ebikunganyiziddwa okuva ku kyuma ky'okozesa.

Engeri y'okujjuzaamu ebikwata ku Pulogulaamu?

  • Mu kitundu kino, mulimu sitetimenti ez'enjawulo n'ebiyinza okuddibwamu.
  • Ebiyinza okuddibwamu (n'obubonero obubiriko) mulimu; Ebikwata ku nteekateeka tebimala (0), tewali kugezaako (1), bagezaako (2), bakulaakulana (3), bawanguzi (4).
  • Nyiga ku ppeesa erinnyonnyola embeera ekitongole kyo mwe kiri mu kiseera ekyo mu mirimu gy'akyo n'ebikwata ku pulogulaamu/enteekateeka y'okugya abantu mu bwavu, okuvujjirira pulogulaamu, kwossa okulondoola n'okupima emitendera.
  • Omugatte gw'obubonero gujja kwegatta gwokka ku buli kibuuzo era gugende butereevu awali Ebivuddemu.

Engeri y'okutaputamu ebivuddemu?

  • Ekigendererwa ky'ebitongole ebikozesa enkola ya CLD (enkola ey'abantu okwenyigira obutereevu mu nkulaakulana yaabwe) kirina kuba kya kulaba nga bamalayo byonna ebiri mu nteekateeka eno, ebigya mu mbeera yaabwe, pulogulaamu zaabwe n'emirimu gyabwe.
  • Si buli kibuuzo ekituukibwako nti kikola ku pulogulaamu zonna, wabula we kisangibwa nga kyetaagisa, ekifo ekyeraga ku lupimo kisobola okuyamba ebitongole okumanya wa we basobola okwongera amaanyi.
  • Omugatte gw'ebivuddemu; gulaga ebivudde ku buli kibuuzo ekibuuziddwa.
  • Okumenyamenya: Ekitundu ky'ebikwata ku nteekateeka kirimu okumenyamenya ebitundu eby'enjawulo okuli ebibuuzo ebirina okuddibwamu okuva ku A - G (OMULI EBIKWATA KU NTEEKATEEKA CLD) n'emitendera emirala (omuli okulondoola n'okupima ebikoleddwa, n'okuvujjirira enteekateeka, nga biraga engeri ebintu bino gye biyinza okukolamu mu kitongole. Okumenyamenya kuno kuli ku ffoomu y'olupimo ku ppeesa ery'okuna n'eryokutaano okusobola okulaba nga ebintu bino eby'omugaso tebibula.
  • Fuba okulaba ng'okubaganya ebirowoozo ku bivuddemu, n'abantu b'omu kitundu n'abakozi b'ekitongole abeetabyemu, oluvanyuma lw'okukozesa akatiba kano, wamu nabo. Mukubaganye ebirowoozo ku birina okusoosowazibwa ne bye mulina okukola okusobola okufuula pulogulaamu yammwe ey'abantu nga musinziira ku bivudde mu kubala kw'akatiba.
